# 👋 About Me

I'm Prasad Adhav, an **AI Engineer & Research Software Developer** with a Ph.D. from the University of Luxembourg and 7+ years of experience developing computational software, machine learning systems, and data-driven applications.

My expertise spans **Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models (LLMs), Machine Learning, Data Engineering, High-Performance Computing (HPC), and Scientific Software Development**. I design and develop AI-powered applications, ML pipelines, and computational tools using **Python, C++, R, SQL, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and modern AI frameworks**.

I have experience building **LLM-powered applications, agentic AI workflows, ETL pipelines, predictive models, computer vision systems, and production-oriented AI solutions**. My background in HPC and numerical simulation enables me to solve complex engineering problems through scalable software architectures and data-driven approaches.

I am passionate about bridging **AI, software engineering, and scientific innovation** through collaborative projects that transform research concepts into practical solutions.

My academic background includes a Ph.D. in Computational Science from the University of Luxembourg, an Erasmus Mundus Master's degree in Computational Mechanics from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Barcelona) and École Centrale de Nantes (France), and a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Pune.
